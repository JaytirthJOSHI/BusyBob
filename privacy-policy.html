1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Account Data: Email address, name, profile information, and authentication tokens
  • Contact Information: Email for communications and support
  • Age Information: Age verification for COPPA compliance and Kid Mode features

Academic Information:

  • StudentVue Data: Grades, assignments, attendance records, class schedules, and school information (with your authorization)
  • Canvas LMS Data: Course information, assignments, grades, discussions, announcements, and calendar events (with your authorization)
  • Tasks and Assignments: Personal tasks, deadlines, priorities, and completion status
  • Academic Goals: Study plans, productivity targets, and progress tracking

Music and Entertainment:

  • Spotify Data: Listening history, playlist preferences, music analytics, and focus session data (when connected)
  • Music Preferences: Mood-based playlist selections and productivity music patterns
  • Focus Sessions: Duration, music played, and productivity correlations

Wellness and Productivity:

  • Mood Data: Daily mood ratings, mood tags, and emotional wellness tracking
  • Journal Entries: Personal reflections, thoughts, and experiences you choose to record
  • Usage Patterns: App interaction patterns, feature usage, and productivity metrics

AI Assistant Interactions:

  • Conversation Data: Messages exchanged with the AI Assistant
  • Action Approvals: Your responses to AI-suggested actions and tasks
  • Context Information: Data used to personalize AI responses (anonymized when possible)

Technical Information:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: Page views, feature usage, session duration, and error logs
  • IP Address: For security, fraud prevention, and geographic analytics
  • Cookies and Local Storage: For authentication, preferences, and functionality

We use the information we collect to:

Core Service Provision:

  • Provide and maintain our academic management services
  • Sync your academic data across platforms (StudentVue, Canvas)
  • Enable music integration and mood-based recommendations
  • Power the AI Assistant with relevant context and personalization
  • Generate insights and analytics about your academic performance and wellness

Communication and Support:

  • Send important updates about your account and our services
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send educational content and productivity tips (with your consent)

Improvement and Development:

  • Improve our application and develop new features
  • Train and improve AI Assistant capabilities
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Conduct research on educational productivity and wellness

Security and Compliance:

  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations and educational regulations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other policies

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these specific circumstances:

With Your Consent:

  • When you explicitly authorize data sharing
  • For features you specifically request or enable

Educational Institution Integration:

  • StudentVue: We access your school's portal with your credentials to sync academic data
  • Canvas LMS: We connect to your learning management system to retrieve course information
  • Data is synced according to your school's policies and your authorization

Music Service Integration:

  • Spotify: We access your music data through Spotify's API with your OAuth authorization
  • Music recommendations and playlists are processed according to Spotify's privacy policy

Service Providers:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication services (data processing agreement in place)
  • AI Services: For AI Assistant functionality (anonymized when possible)
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Hosting and data storage services with appropriate security measures

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law, court order, or government request
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
  • In connection with legal proceedings or investigations
  • To comply with educational regulations (FERPA, COPPA, etc.)

Business Transactions:

  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)
  • Data protection obligations would transfer to the new entity

4. Data Security and Anti-Abuse Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information and prevent unauthorized interactions:

Encryption and Transport Security:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption algorithms
  • Database connections are secured and encrypted
  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive communications and AI interactions

Access Controls and Authentication:

  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Regular access audits and permission reviews
  • Continuous authentication monitoring for suspicious login patterns
  • Device fingerprinting to detect unauthorized access attempts
  • Session invalidation for compromised or suspicious accounts

Infrastructure Security:

  • Secure cloud hosting with SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and patch management
  • Network firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • DDoS protection and traffic filtering systems
  • Real-time threat monitoring and automated response systems

Anti-Abuse and Interaction Protection:

  • Rate Limiting: API calls and user interactions are rate-limited to prevent abuse
  • Behavioral Analysis: AI-powered systems detect unusual interaction patterns
  • Content Filtering: Automated detection and blocking of harmful or inappropriate content
  • Integration Security: All third-party connections are validated and monitored
  • Bot Detection: Advanced systems identify and block automated interactions
  • Spam Prevention: Multi-layered filters prevent unwanted communications
  • Account Isolation: Suspicious accounts are automatically quarantined

Data Protection Measures:

  • Regular automated backups with secure storage
  • Data integrity checks and monitoring
  • Incident response procedures for security breaches
  • Employee training on data privacy and security practices
  • Data Loss Prevention (DLP) systems monitor for unauthorized data access
  • Anomaly Detection: AI systems identify unusual data access patterns
  • Breach Containment: Automated systems isolate and contain potential breaches

User Protection Controls:

  • Privacy Controls: Granular settings to control data sharing and interactions
  • Blocking and Reporting: Tools to block unwanted interactions and report abuse
  • Communication Filtering: AI-powered filters for inappropriate communications
  • Safe Mode Options: Enhanced security settings for vulnerable users
  • Parental Controls: Additional protections for minor users
  • Emergency Lockdown: Immediate account protection in case of compromise

⚠️ Security Notice

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We continuously monitor and improve our security infrastructure. If you notice any suspicious activity, immediately contact our security team at security@busybob.app.

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for abuse, unauthorized access, or malicious interactions. Violations result in immediate account suspension and potential legal action.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have significant control over your personal information. Your rights include:

Access and Portability:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you
  • Data Export: Download your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Account Dashboard: View and manage your data through your account settings

Correction and Update:

  • Profile Updates: Modify your account information and preferences
  • Data Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Integration Management: Update or re-authorize connected services

Deletion and Opt-Out:

  • Account Deletion: Delete your entire account and associated data
  • Selective Deletion: Remove specific data types (e.g., mood logs, journal entries)
  • Service Disconnection: Disconnect from integrated services (Spotify, StudentVue, Canvas)
  • Communication Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

Control and Restriction:

  • Privacy Settings: Control what data is collected and how it's used
  • AI Interactions: Limit or disable AI Assistant data usage
  • Analytics Opt-Out: Disable usage analytics and tracking
  • Third-Party Restrictions: Control data sharing with integrated services

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  • Account Settings: Most privacy controls are available in your account dashboard
  • Contact Support: Email privacy@busybob.app for specific requests
  • Response Time: We respond to privacy requests within 30 days
  • Verification: We may need to verify your identity for security purposes

6. Data Retention

Active Account Data:

  • We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active
  • Academic data is retained to provide continuous service and analytics
  • Music and wellness data is kept to improve personalized recommendations

Account Deletion:

  • Standard Deletion: Most personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Anonymized Analytics: Some usage patterns may be retained in anonymized form for service improvement
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes

Specific Data Types:

  • AI Conversations: Stored for service improvement; can be deleted upon request
  • Academic Integration Data: Deleted when you disconnect services or delete your account
  • Music Data: Spotify integration data is removed when you disconnect Spotify
  • Security Logs: Retained for up to 2 years for security and fraud prevention

Backup and Recovery:

  • Backups are retained for disaster recovery purposes
  • Backup data follows the same deletion schedules as primary data
  • Encrypted backups are securely deleted according to our retention schedule

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Age Requirements:

  • General Use: Users must be 13 or older to create a standard account
  • Kid Mode: Available for users under 13 with verifiable parental consent
  • Parental Supervision: Parents/guardians must supervise and authorize account creation for minors

Enhanced Protections for Minors:

  • Limited Data Collection: We collect only information necessary for the service
  • No Behavioral Advertising: We don't use children's data for advertising purposes
  • Content Filtering: Automatic filtering of inappropriate content and features
  • AI Safety: Enhanced monitoring and safety controls for AI Assistant interactions
  • Secure Integrations: Additional privacy controls for educational platform connections

Parental Rights and Controls:

  • Access: Parents can review their child's account information
  • Control: Parents can modify privacy settings and connected services
  • Deletion: Parents can request deletion of their child's account and data
  • Communication: Direct contact channel for parental inquiries and concerns

Educational Context:

  • Academic integrations follow educational privacy laws (FERPA)
  • School-authorized use may have different privacy provisions
  • We work with educational institutions to ensure appropriate privacy protections
